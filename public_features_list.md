# Slot Theory — Public Features

Slot Theory is a fast, neon-soaked auto-battler / tower-defense hybrid where you draft one card at a time, build a filthy synergy engine, and watch your setup tear through 20 escalating waves.

## Core Features

### Draft Your Build
- Choose one card at a time from a rotating draft
- Build your run around towers, modifiers, and nasty synergies
- Shape completely different archetypes from run to run

### Tactical Auto-Battler Combat
- Place towers into fixed slots and let the wave play out automatically
- Focus on buildcraft, setup, targeting, and long-run decisions instead of constant clicking
- Chase cleaner boards, smarter combos, and stronger wave answers

### 5 Distinct Towers
- Rapid Shooter
- Heavy Cannon
- Marker Tower
- Arc Emitter
- Rift Sapper

Each tower supports different modifier combinations and surge interactions, giving every build its own rhythm and personality.

### 10 Stackable Modifiers
Mix damage scaling, chain effects, status synergy, split fire, range changes, cooldown abuse, and more to create specialized builds and ridiculous proc chains.

### Global Surge System
- Fill the Global Surge meter through combat
- Manually trigger a huge build-driven power event when the moment is right
- Each surge takes on a named archetype based on your build
- Visual effects, payload, and feel shift depending on what your run is actually doing

### Big Combat Feedback
- Punchy hit FX, kill bursts, chain arcs, and damage numbers
- Major proc callouts for your nastiest moments
- Low-life tension feedback when a run starts getting dangerous
- Strong visual payoff when your build comes online

## Modes

### Campaign Mode — The Fracture Circuit
Push through a 4-stage campaign with escalating battlefield mandates. Each stage introduces a new constraint or pressure layer, forcing you to adapt instead of sleepwalking through the same build every time.

### Skirmish Mode
Jump straight into free-play runs on individual maps and difficulties.

### Endless Mode
Clear wave 20, then keep going into Endless to see how long your build can survive.

### Tutorial Run
A dedicated scripted tutorial teaches the core systems without dumping a wall of nonsense on the player.

## Progression and Replay Value

### Unlockable Content
Progress through the game to unlock additional content, including new towers and modifiers.

### Difficulty Modes
Play on Easy, Normal, or Hard depending on how much pain you’re in the mood for.

### Achievements
- 16 in-game achievements
- Persistent progression tracking
- Steam achievement forwarding when available

### Leaderboards
- Personal best tracking
- Global Steam leaderboard support
- Every run can stand on its own
- Run-specific build names help your best disasters look memorable

## Presentation

### Fully Procedural Music
Slot Theory features an adaptive procedural soundtrack that reacts to tension, pacing, and combat state instead of just looping the same dead background track forever.

### Strong Visual Identity
- Neon synthwave-inspired UI
- Distinct tower and enemy visuals
- Flashy surge presentation
- Clean readability-focused combat effects

### Build Identity Everywhere
The game highlights what your run actually became through surge archetypes, combat feedback, naming, and leaderboard presentation.

## Short Pitch

Build a monstrous tower setup. Draft brutal modifier combos. Trigger huge surges. Survive 20 waves or push into Endless.

Slot Theory is about watching a run evolve from “this might work” into “what the hell have I built?”
