Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Publisher: 7ants Studios

This policy applies to the game Slot Theory, available on Steam. It explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Data we collect

  • Display name (optional). If you choose to submit a score to the global leaderboard you will be prompted to enter a short display name (max 10 characters). This name is stored on our leaderboard server (Supabase) alongside your score, wave reached, and run statistics. You can skip this step - no name means no global submission.
  • Run statistics. When a global score is submitted we store: score, wave reached, lives remaining, total damage dealt, total kills, run duration, tower/modifier build, and game version. No personally identifying information beyond the display name you chose.
  • Local save data. High scores and settings are saved to your local machine (%APPDATA%\Godot\app_userdata\Slot Theory\). If Steam Cloud is enabled, this data is also synced via Steam Remote Storage so it is available across your devices. Valve's privacy policy governs Steam Cloud storage.
  • Steam data. If you play through Steam, Steam collects usage data governed by Valve's Privacy Policy. We receive your Steam display name for leaderboard display (Steam leaderboard path only) and achievement state - no email, purchase history, or other Steam account data.

2. Data we do NOT collect

  • Email addresses or contact information
  • Payment information (handled entirely by Valve/Steam)
  • Location, device identifiers, or IP addresses
  • Gameplay telemetry or analytics beyond submitted leaderboard entries
  • Any data from users under 13 (the game has no account system or chat)

3. How we use the data

Leaderboard entries (display name + run statistics) are used solely to display the global leaderboard inside the game. They are never sold, shared with third parties for marketing, or used for any purpose other than operating the leaderboard.

4. Data retention and deletion

Leaderboard entries are retained indefinitely so your best run stays on the board. To request deletion of your submitted scores, contact us at the address below. Local save data can be deleted by removing the files from %APPDATA%\Godot\app_userdata\Slot Theory\.

5. Third-party services

  • Supabase - leaderboard backend. Data is stored in the EU. Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Valve / Steam - game distribution, achievements, Steam Cloud. Steam Privacy Policy.

6. Children's privacy

Slot Theory is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted data, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the game after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
